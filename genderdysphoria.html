    YouTubers Play List

    Stef Stafani
    Stef Stanfani is a YouTuber and professional make up artist whose most popular video describes her Waardenburg Syndrome. She came out as transgender in another one of her most viewed videos. In this specific video she talks about her experiences with Gender Dysphoria.

    Connor O'Keefe
    Connor O'Keefe is a trans youtuber with a love for his camera. He has proudly posted a short movie that you can find in his YouTube channel. In this video talks about how to help someone you know that is experiencing Gender Dysphoria.

    Ryan Cassata
    Ryan Cassata is an American musician, public speaker, writer, filmmaker, and actor. In this video he gives advice and tips on how to deal with gender dysphoria.

    Trans Boys United
    Trans Boys United is a group of teenage trans boys from around the world. They offer a support and advice for people(specifically trans people) who are experiencing or have experienced Gender Dysphoria.

    Francois C
    Francois C is an FTM transgender from the Netherlands. His goal is to help and inspire other people. This video describes in detail what Gender Dysphoria feels like and how it affects him throughout his daily lifestyle.

    Maya
    Maya is a proud trans woman who hopes to inspire people in the trans community and spread awareness to those who are curious. This video shows her transition process.

    Miles Chronicles
    Mileschronicles is a tran Non-binary YouTuber. He creates comedic videos and vlog entries for his channel from which he bases the majority of his comedy on his sexuality and gender identity.

    Milo Stewart
    Milo's pronouns are they/them. He was named "YouTube's most controversial trans vlogger" by The Daily Dot. This video explains their experiences and gives advice on Online Dating for Trans/Non-Binary People.

    Jammi dodger
    Jamie likes to talk about trans stuff and not trans stuff, he hopes you can learn stuff and laugh. This video takes you through Jamie's surgery experiences when it came to his transition.

    BeautyWith Trinity
    Trinity created this channel when she decided to document her transition as a transgender female, as well as to make a community where people can feel themselves and embrace their inner and outer beauty. Her videos range from makeup tutorials to storytime videos. This specific video however shows Trinity and her cousin reacting to Trinity before she went through her transition to a female.
