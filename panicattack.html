    Techniques to help yourself through panic attacks:

    • ☀ Recognize the signs of a panic attack. Are you hyperventilating? Trembling? Learn what your body does when you are having a panic attack.
    • ☀ Realize that you are having a panic attack. You are not dying, the world is not ending.
    • ☀ If possible, go to a place you can be alone.
    • ☀ B R E A T H E. Inhale deeply from the bottom of your stomach. It can help to visualize a bubble floating down to the bottom of your tummy when you inhale, and out your mouth when you exhale.
    • ☀ Pick a color and look for things of that color in the room
    • ☀ Pick a word or your name and see how many other or small words you can make from the letters in it
    • ☀ If possible, describe your surroundings out loud in detail, including sights, sounds, smells, and temperature
    • ☀ Run cool or warm (but not too cold or hot) water over your hands or take a cool or wam bath or shower
    • ☀ Touch and hold objects around you. Compare the feel, weight, temperature, textures, colors, and materials
    • ☀ Dance to a nice song
